    • I finished my 5 years university studies in 1981 at the University of Tirana, Faculty of Nature Sciences, Physics branch with speciality Physician. I worked as a lecturer at the Physics department of the Faculty of Nature Science in the University of Elbasan. In 1992, I defended my dissertation for " Doctorate Candidate at Physics Sciences " with the topic: "Physical study of carbonic materials and composites with two and three components for laboratory and industrial production of the self-baked electrode provision." and in 1993, it was converted as "Doctor of Science". I have academic and scientific activity and in 1994 I have been rewarded the title: "Asst. Prof" and in 1999 as "Professor".
    • I have had important duties in the higher education field such as: Physician at the Laboratory of Carbon Materials at the Metallurgy Combine, Director of the Physics Department at the Faculty of Nature Sciences in the University of Elbasan, Rector of the Elbasan University, Vice Rector, Director of Administrative Board of the University, etc. I have several publications, textbooks, scientific papers, monography, references, coordinator of projects in different countries, reviewer, examiner, supervisor in different thesis, etc.
